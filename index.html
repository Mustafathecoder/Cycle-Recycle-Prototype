About Us

Cycle Recycle is a platform that helps you recycle common items like plastic bottles, bags, and cans, while earning rewards for each contribution. Let's work together to reduce waste and build a sustainable future.

How It Works

  • Collect recyclables like plastic bottles, cans, and bags.
  • Drop them off at our partnered recycling centers.
  • Earn points for your contribution.
  • Redeem points for eco-friendly rewards or donate them to causes.

Rewards

Earn points and choose from a variety of rewards, such as gift cards, eco-products, or make a donation to environmental charities!
